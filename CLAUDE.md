The ultimate goal of this project is to create demo apps for showing how Solid-DToU works. I want to use it to demonstrate the technical advantage compared to other approaches (e.g. plain WAC/ACP, SAI, ODRL, etc). I'll use it during Solid Symposium (SoSy) 2026, and probably reuse it later in other venues or the general public later.

I have a Solid service capable of performing DToU reasoning through the defined APIs, at http://localhost:3000.

Everything is developed by me. Please find relevant information at `TechDetails.md` (may not be complete). `DToUDistinction.md` describes the advantage of DToU compared to others.

If you need further information after exploring relevant docs or code, just ask.
